I am a senior undergraduate student at the University of Connecticut majoring in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Mathematics. My research interests are hydrodynamics stability, optimization, nonlinear stability analysis, and CFD. I am an undergraduate researcher in the *FLUids, rEduction, Nonlinearity, and Turbulence Lab* at UConn, where I work with Professor Chang Liu on nonlinear and time-varying flow stability theories. In Fall 2026, I will pursue my PhD at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology's *FU lab for computational physics and fluid mechanics*, advised by Professor Lin Fu.

You can also find my work on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=BqbfWaYAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN).
